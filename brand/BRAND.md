# AstrOS Brand Guidelines

These guidelines cover the **AstrOS name and logo**. They are separate from the
GPLv3 license that covers the source code. You can freely use the software while still being
asked to follow the rules below for the brand.

---

## 1. The name

- Always written **AstrOS** - capital `A`, lowercase `str`, capital `OS`, no space. `astros` in places where lowercase is required.
- Not: "Astros", "AstroOS", "ASTROS", "Astros Linux", "astrOS".

## 4. Trademark policy

The goal of this policy is simple: **let people talk about, use, and share official AstrOS freely,
while making sure the name only ever points at the real, unmodified thing.**
Because AstrOS ships signed images, a modified build is not the same system!!
**It must not carry the same name.**

### 4.1 Feel free to
- Use our Brand in social media / news articles while pointing at our real thing.
- State factual compatibility like "runs on AstrOS"
- Link to the project and use the logo to link back to official AstrOS resources.

### 4.2 Don't
- Distribute a **modified** build, remix, or fork under the AstrOS name or logo.
  (You're free to distribute modified code under the GPL - just pick a different
  name and remove the AstrOS logo. This is the same "rename your fork" rule
  most FOSS projects use.)
  They may combine their own name/trademark with one of the following AstrOS Mark tag-lines (or equivalent):
  - Inspired by
  - Based on
  - Derived from
- Use the mark in a way that misleads people
  (e.g. presenting a modified image as official AstrOS).
- Register AstrOS-confusing domains, handles, or trademarks.
- Use our name in connection with illegal activities
